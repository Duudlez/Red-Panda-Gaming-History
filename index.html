About me!

I'm a 36-year-old gaming ehtusiast who has been passionate about video games for over 25 years. My journey started with the Game Boy and SNES, which sparked a lifelong love for gaming. Over the years, I've explored countless games across various platforms, from retro classics to modern masterpieces.

Creating a website about gaming history is a personal hobby of mine, born out of a desire to share the fascinating stories behind the games, consoles, and industry milestones that have shaped this incredible medium. Gaming isn't just about playing-it's a rich tapestry of innovation, culture, and creativity. My site is a small space where I can document and celebrate the evolution of gaming in a way that's both informative and enjoyable.

Through this project, I want to highlight the moments that defined gaming history-wether it's the 8-bit char of early consoles, the breakthroughs in storytelling and graphic, or the enduring appeal of iconic franchises. My aim is to create a resource that's fun for fellow enthusiasts and accessible to newcomers who want to learn more about gaming's legacy.

This website is more than just a collection of facts; it's a reflection of my personal passion for the medium and my appreciation for how it has grown and impacted millions of lives. By sharing bits and pieces of gaming history, I hope to inspire others to explore this amazing world, appreciate it's roots, and maybe even discover something new about the games they love. It's a small, heartfelt project-but one that means a lot to me as a lifelong gamer.
